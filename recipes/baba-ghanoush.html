Baba Ghanoush

This recipe is a tasty Baba Ghanoush!

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp. almond butter
  • ½ cup (125g) peach vegan yogurt (like Alpro)
  • ½ cup (120ml) almond milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) + 2 tbsp. maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 355°F (180°C) and grease (length) round cake tin or line it with baking paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond butter and yogurt until smooth, then gradually add in the almond milk and maple syrup. Finally, mix in the lemon juice and vanilla.
